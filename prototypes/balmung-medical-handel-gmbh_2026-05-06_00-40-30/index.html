Tender growth needs software that keeps pace with orders

Balmung is growing tenders, product data, and in-house Java software at the same time. We would help make this work faster, cleaner, and easier to scale.

We saw the Lead Tender Management and Tender Data roles, plus Java work on your planning and control software. That usually means tender volume is moving faster than manual tools can support.

Our read

The signal says Balmung is building a stronger tender engine, not just adding sales people. Tender work needs clean product data, RFQ inputs, prices, documents, and delivery dates to match. If these steps stay split across Excel, email, and the Java system, the team can win work but spend too much time proving each bid. The risk is slower replies, more rework, and less room for the new tender lead to manage strategy.

What we'd do
