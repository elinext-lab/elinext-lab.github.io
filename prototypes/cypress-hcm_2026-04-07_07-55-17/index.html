Current Flow vs. Cypress Flow

The strongest commercial fix is not a full rebuild. It is one contractor workspace that sits above the existing stack and removes the moments where users get lost, logged out, or forced to guess which code belongs to which system.

Why this matters to Sales

  • Strategic clients hear one story in the pitch but workers feel another in the product handoff.
  • Login and timecard friction becomes support cost, slower starts, and softer account confidence.
  • A branded mobile layer gives Cypress a cleaner enterprise narrative without replacing every backend system.
Make process quality visible. That is easier to sell than a stack of portals and vendor logins.
