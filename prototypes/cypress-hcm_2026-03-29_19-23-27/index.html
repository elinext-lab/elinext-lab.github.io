The wedge is operational, not cosmetic

Cypress does not need to replace Paylocity or rebuild every back-office workflow. The faster move is to wrap the existing systems with a branded mobile layer that handles navigation, state, alerts, and exception recovery better.

That gives Brett a cleaner product story to sell: not just staffing and compliance services, but a Cypress journey that stays coherent from the public career portal through payroll.
