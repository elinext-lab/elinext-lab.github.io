Prototype Direction

Build a sponsor-side activation layer, not a full rewrite.

This concept sits around the existing regulated study app. It reduces rescue work for coordinators while giving operations a cleaner view of who is stalled, why they stalled, and what support already happened.

Participant preflight

Confirm invite, device readiness, reminder settings, and accessibility preferences before login failure becomes a ticket.

Invite recovery

Replace dead-end QR or password loops with text-link resend, one-tap reset, and visible site contact paths.

Shorter guided training

Convert dense trainer copy into progressive, saveable, visual coaching that gets participants to first entry faster.

Ops and compliance visibility

Show stalled activations, rescue attempts, and first-entry status to coordinators and sponsor-side operations.
