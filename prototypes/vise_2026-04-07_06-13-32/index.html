Why This Angle

High-leverage because it converts AI insight into advisor action

Public Vise messaging already covers tax-efficient transitions, trade transparency, macro awareness, and client-ready summaries. The missing layer is a mobile shell that compresses those outputs into one decision surface, especially now that Vise is broadening the workflow with overlays and crypto access.

01 Queue the highest-stakes account work first

Rank items by client sensitivity, tax impact, transition deadline, and urgency so an advisor knows what deserves attention before opening a laptop.

02 Attach the “why” to every action

Bring trade rationale, concentration context, and macro exposure directly into the card so explanation and approval happen in one motion.

03 Ship client-ready communication from the same flow

Move from insight to a reusable summary or approved message without forcing the advisor through separate desktop views.

Elinext Angle

A mobile companion that makes Vise Intelligence feel immediate

This concept is intentionally narrow: not a broad redesign, but a fast pilot that helps advisors clear transition blockers, explain trades, and send client-ready answers from one phone-first workflow.
