Our Read

Where the load is likely landing

The hiring signal tells us Cintra is still adding operational capacity while the product and service base grows. Public reviews show a split picture. Users like easy payslip access when the flow works, but others report login friction, blank screens, and payroll confusion. For a Head of IT and Cyber security, that usually means support noise, identity friction, and release risk landing on one team. If that stays open, trust in self-service drops fast and payroll issues turn into security and brand issues too.
