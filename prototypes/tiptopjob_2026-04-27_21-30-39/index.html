Proposal brief for Mike Dauncey

Support TipTopJob's job board while it keeps growing

TipTopJob already serves jobseekers and recruiters across many countries. This brief focuses on keeping the web platform faster, clearer, and easier to support as data grows.

We saw the 1st Line IT Support signal and your live job-board activity. That points to a platform that needs steady care while recruiters and candidates keep using it.

Focus for a first sprint

  • Search and job alerts
  • CV upload and profile flows
  • Recruiter posting and CV search
  • Performance, QA, and support reports

Our read

The support signal tells us TipTopJob is keeping a live job-board engine stable, not just publishing content. Your site spans many countries, industries, alerts, CVs, and recruiter products. That breadth can create slow fixes, hard QA, and unclear support data. If it stays that way, paid posting, candidate trust, and any investment talks can lose speed.
