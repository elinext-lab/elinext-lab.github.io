Gotion x Elinext

A stronger service layer for Gotion's connected energy rollout

Gotion is scaling EV, ESS, and home energy products. The hard part is not only shipment. It is support, training, warranty, and device data that keep customers confident after install.

We saw Gotion hiring an after-sales engineer to build the service system for Europe and Africa. At the same time, the Gendome app keeps adding warranty, message, and AI support features. That points to one need: the product and service stack must scale together.

Our read

The signal says Gotion is moving from product shipment to regional service ownership. That shift gets harder when one team must handle ESS projects, home systems, field training, warranty cases, and app-driven support at once. Gendome's release history shows the software layer is now part of the service promise, not a side tool. If that layer stays uneven, customers wait longer, complaints loop back to engineers, and each new market launch costs more to support.
