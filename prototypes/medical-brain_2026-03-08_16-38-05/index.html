Real Reviews, Real Frustration

PIN Forgotten Every Login

Multiple Samsung/Android users report the app forgets their PIN on every restart, forcing a reset cycle.

"The app forgets my 4-number PIN every time I attempt the login. The only way to use the app is to reset the PIN every time."
— 1★ Google Play, Sept 2025 (3 thumbs up)
💥

Crash Before Login

The app crashes before users can even enter their phone number — zero chance of onboarding.

"The app crashes before I can even put in my phone number."
— 1★ Google Play, Dec 2025
🔔

Unstoppable Loud Notifications

Notification sounds override phone silent mode. No way to turn them off — users threaten to uninstall.

"Whether phone is on silent or not, the app makes a horrifically loud PING. There is no way to turn it off."
— 1★ App Store, Jan 2024
🚪

Onboarding Dead Ends

Users get stuck on privacy policy pages, can't create accounts, or never receive verification codes.

"Poor documentation. Not clear how to create an account."
— 1★ Google Play, Feb 2024 (2 thumbs up)
📱

Android Parity Issues

Samsung-specific bugs and historical iOS-only support erode trust among Android users.

"It works sometimes and then sometimes not... in the beginning it was only working with Apple phones."
— 3★ Google Play, Jul 2021 (7 thumbs up)
🗑️

No Message Control

Patients can't delete old messages — the most-engaged negative review with 12 thumbs up.

"I can't delete old messages, and most of what it wants is frankly no one's business."
