How the concept plugs into existing trial operations

The right implementation pattern is lightweight: sit above existing study systems, pull operational status into one mobile workflow, and push timestamped actions back into the right compliance and data channels.

Site + Patient Ops Visit readiness, travel status, caregiver logistics, home-visit planning.
Biomarker Collection Barcode confirmation, chain-of-custody, freezer and courier events.
EDC + ePRO Status Surface only missing steps, overdue forms, and unresolved data blockers.
Lab + CRO Visibility Specialty-lab receipt, assay-progress cues, vendor escalation routing.
Audit Trail Event log for TMF-linked documentation and study-team review.

Elinext can help turn this into a study-ready mobile layer.

The strongest wedge is not a broad patient app. It is a focused operations companion for coordinators, field teams, and biomarker stakeholders that makes DMD study execution easier during a readout-heavy growth period.
