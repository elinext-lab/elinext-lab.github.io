Why this concept fits Sentry specifically

This is not a generic mobile dashboard. It is shaped around the public signals around Sentry’s current org and product: mobile SDK investment, TPM buildout, and customer demand for cleaner post-release decision loops.

Built for Meng Wang’s org problem

The live TPM role calls out delivery-health metrics, risk visibility, and roadmap confidence. Pulse turns those goals into a release-by-release mobile briefing.

Built for the mobile SDK team

The Android SDK hiring language highlights profiling, session replay, and next-generation performance monitoring. Pulse exposes those signals as fast decisions instead of buried context.

Built for impact-first alerting

Reddit discussion keeps landing on the same rule: alert on impact, not everything. The prototype makes that the product default.
