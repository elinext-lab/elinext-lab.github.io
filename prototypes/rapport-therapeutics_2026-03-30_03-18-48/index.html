Design Principles

Built for regulated teams, not generic “task management”

The concept stays intentionally narrow. It does not try to replace core clinical systems. It gives the people responsible for delivery and compliance a faster mobile layer on top of them.

Public Trigger
Rapport’s live role mentions lab data, GxP, cloud, cybersecurity, and cross-functional scaling.
Audit Trail Every action leaves a clean trace

Missing docs, note-to-file approvals, and escalations stay attached to the event instead of being reconstructed later.

Mobile First Fast response off desktop

Leaders can review and route issues while moving between meetings, sites, or bicoastal time zones.

Role Aware Different actions for different owners

Clinical ops, regulatory, IT, QA, and vendors each see the right next step instead of one oversized queue.

Pragmatic Adds leverage without rip-and-replace

The idea complements validated systems and makes them easier to work with, rather than forcing a full platform rewrite.
