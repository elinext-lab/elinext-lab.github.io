Proposed Flow

One ranked incident, one owner, one audit trail.

This concept keeps the surface intentionally narrow. It does not try to rebuild the full WMS or yard system on mobile. It gives supervisors the exact controls they need when service risk starts to rise away from a desk.

Risk engine creates one mobile incident card Dock delay, missing POD, ETA drift, and yard congestion are merged into a single decision object.
Document state is visible without opening another system OCR confidence, latest upload, missing fields, and approval blockers sit next to the operational issue.
Supervisor clears the issue in seconds Approve with note, reroute to another dock, request a rescan, or escalate with full context already attached.
