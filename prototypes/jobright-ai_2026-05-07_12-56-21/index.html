Our read

The AI agent hiring signal says Jobright is scaling from advice to action. Orion and Jobright Agent must judge fit, edit resumes, work with ATS pages, and stop when data is weak. The bottleneck is likely the system around the model: fresh jobs, source quality, low latency, and tests for non-repeatable AI output. If that stays thin, users will see wrong locations, weak autofill, or unsafe apply steps.
