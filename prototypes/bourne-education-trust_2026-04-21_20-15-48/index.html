Our Read

The department hire tells us BET is protecting delivery in a high-prep subject while pushing digital work across schools. That usually creates gaps between trust plans and daily practice. Art, Design and Technology teams need shared resources, clear handover, and fast local support. If that stays manual, leaders spend time chasing files, cover plans, and onboarding details. That slows school improvement and adds pressure on BET Digital.

We would run a scoped end-to-end project. The aim is one clean delivery layer around BET Digital, staffing continuity, and school operations.
