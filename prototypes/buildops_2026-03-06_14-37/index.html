How It Works

Architecture, not more servers

The fix is structural. An offline-first pipeline handles the reality of commercial job sites — not just happy-path WiFi.

📱

Local-First Storage

Photos are saved to device storage immediately. No upload required to confirm capture. The tech always has their photos, regardless of signal.

🗜️

Smart Compression

AI-driven compression reduces 8MB photos to 2MB while preserving nameplate readability. 76% less data to upload means faster sync and less battery drain.

🔄

Background Sync

When connectivity returns, photos sync automatically in the background. No manual retry. No spinner. The tech keeps working while uploads happen silently.

🏷️

AI Auto-Tagging

On-device ML identifies equipment models, nameplates, and photo context. Photos are auto-organized by equipment type and work order phase (before/after).

📋

Offline Work Orders

Full work order data cached locally. Techs can view job history, previous photos, and equipment details even in dead zones. Changes sync when signal returns.

🔋

Battery-Conscious

Adaptive sync scheduling based on battery level and connectivity quality. Upload intensity scales down when battery is low to protect the tech's workday.
