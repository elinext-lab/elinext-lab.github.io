Proposal brief for Terra

Turn Terra's project video push into a stronger buyer journey

Saw Terra is hiring a videographer to show developments, progress, amenities, campaigns, and milestone moments. That points to better video and a stronger digital path for each project story.

Our Read

The videographer role tells us Terra is scaling visual content. It spans sales, leasing, investor relations, and brand campaigns.
