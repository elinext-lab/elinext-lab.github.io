Elinext Proposal brief for Northpoint Commercial Finance

Scale dealer growth without adding more manual handoffs

Northpoint already gives dealers and manufacturers flexible inventory finance. The next gain is likely inside the work around applications, credit packages, status updates, and support follow-up.

Saw Northpoint adding Sales Support capacity. That points to more dealer boarding, CRM work, and credit package follow-up. We would help the systems keep pace.

Our read

Adding sales support tells us Northpoint is preparing for more dealer growth, not just more admin. The risk is that AccessCompass, Salesforce flows, CRM notes, and document checks make staff chase the same file in several places. When that happens, new support hires spend time finding status instead of moving deals. The business cost is slower dealer activation and less clean receivables growth.
