Operations Proposal

Helping ENGIBEX staff R&D projects without slowing delivery

You are hiring project engineers while ENGIBEX keeps widening its R&D work. That usually means demand is healthy. It also means hiring, matching, and onboarding can slow delivery.

Your public site shows strong e-powertrain depth and many open roles. We would build one simple workflow. It would track roles, skills, candidate status, and project readiness.

Signal Hiring project engineers and other specialist profiles.
Public product `e-Powertrain expertise` is the clearest productized ENGIBEX offer.
Gap The hiring journey is split across ENGIBEX, Jobposting, and LinkedIn.

Our Read

Where the friction likely sits

The signal points to growth in project delivery, not just recruiting volume. ENGIBEX now needs faster handoff between sales, hiring, and client staffing. That is harder when roles live on external job pages. Skill data then sits in several places. Your e-powertrain work also needs tight matching between niche profiles and live projects. If that matching stays manual, starts slip and managers spend too much time chasing status.
