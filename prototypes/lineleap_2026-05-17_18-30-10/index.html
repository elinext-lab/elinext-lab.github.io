Proposal brief for Peter Chane

Keep LineLeap nights fast while CX demand grows

LineLeap connects guests, venues, tickets, cover, drinks, and partner revenue. At this stage, each small product gap can turn into a live support moment.

Saw LineLeap is adding Customer Experience support for partner and user issues. That points to growth, but also to more pressure on checkout, ticket access, refunds, and venue-night operations.

Why now Support pressure is a product signal. When CX scales, product teams need faster fixes for the repeat issues behind the tickets.

Our read

The CX hire says LineLeap is protecting busy nights for guests and venue partners. The likely bottleneck is not one big feature. It is many small breaks across pass purchase, ticket access, refund requests, and venue staff handoff. If these stay open, support keeps repeating the same fixes. Peter's team loses time that should go to the roadmap.
