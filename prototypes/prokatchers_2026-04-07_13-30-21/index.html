Suggested build slice

A pragmatic v1 for Nathan: ship trust, not bloat

Module 01

Verified recruiter identity

Give each outreach thread a visible recruiter profile, escalation path, and client verification badge before any deeper data request.

Module 02

Staged document vault

Let candidates share resume first and hold back higher-risk items until the right point in the process, with a clear audit trail.

Module 03

Contact controls

Respect preferred channels, quiet hours, and do-not-contact states inside the product instead of relying on manual recruiter memory.

Module 04

Contract-readiness checklist

Turn compliance-heavy placements into a visible progression: credentials, representation, interview, contract review, and ready-to-submit state.

Built for ProKatchers

If the goal is better candidate conversion, trust has to ship first.
