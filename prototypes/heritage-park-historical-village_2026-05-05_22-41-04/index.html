Proposal brief for Heritage Park

Help business development turn visits into booked events

We saw Heritage Park is hiring an Account Executive for Business Development. That points to a push for catered events, venue bookings, group sales, tourism, and film production.

Why now A new sales hire needs clean paths, fast handoffs, and clear buyer data from day one.

Our read

That hire says Heritage Park is building a stronger sales path beyond gate admission. The hard part is not only finding buyers; it is keeping venue leads, Showpass ticket buyers, members, event planners, and internal teams in one clean flow. If the handoff stays split between website forms, ticket widgets, and manual follow-up, good corporate and tourism leads can slow down before a proposal is sent.
