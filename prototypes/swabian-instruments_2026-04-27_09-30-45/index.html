Proposal brief for Swabian Instruments Prepared by Elinext

Help Time Tagger scale across labs and regions

Swabian builds high performance instruments with software toolkits. We would help protect that experience as global demand grows, especially for setup, updates, and remote lab workflows.

Saw Swabian is hiring production and warehouse help while Jian leads China. That points to more shipped instruments, more users, and more support load around Time Tagger and Pulse Streamer software.

Our read

The hiring signal says production and logistics are growing, not just R&D. When more instruments ship, the hard part becomes repeatable setup, clear updates, and support across languages and time zones. Time Tagger v2.20 adds network clients, virtual channels, and PyPI access, so customers have more ways to use the same core engine. If docs, installers, and support tools do not scale with shipments, Jian's China team can lose time on avoidable setup questions.
