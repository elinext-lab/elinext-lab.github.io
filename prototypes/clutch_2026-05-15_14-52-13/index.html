Proposal brief for Clutch

Keep Clutch delivery smooth as the field team grows

Clutch already owns the core platform. The next gain is making each delivery advocate faster, clearer, and less blocked by manual follow-up.

We saw the Customer Service Delivery Advocate hiring signal. That points to more customer handoffs, more delivery routes, and more pressure on the tools behind each appointment.

Our read

The hiring signal says Clutch is scaling the human side of a fully online car journey. That is a good growth sign, but it can expose gaps between checkout, financing, inspection notes, and delivery routing. If advocates need to chase data by hand, each new hire adds cost before adding full capacity. For a CFO, the risk is simple: more headcount, more exceptions, and slower payback on growth.
