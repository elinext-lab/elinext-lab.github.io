A focused engineering proposal for the data, model, and QA work around Venerable's variable annuity platform.

Saw you are hiring an Associate Actuary for assumptions, stress testing, and automation. That points to a practical need: cleaner data paths, safer model changes, and better proof for risk reports.

Our read

Venerable is scaling risk transfer work while adding actuarial depth around assumptions. That means each new block can bring fresh policy data, product rules, hedging inputs, and reporting needs. The hard part is not the actuarial math alone. It is making the data path repeatable, tested, and clear enough for finance, auditors, cedants, and regulators. If that layer stays manual, good actuaries lose time to file work and checks.

What we'd do

We would start with a six-week dedicated squad under your risk or product owner. The aim is one tested flow from raw data to assumption review.
