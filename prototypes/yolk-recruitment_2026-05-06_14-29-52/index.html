Proposal brief for Yolk Recruitment

Make Yolk's delivery flow match its recruitment ambition

Yolk already connects clients and candidates across many specialist markets. The next gain is making the web, service, and reporting flow easier to run every week.

Saw the Agile Delivery Manager and Service and Delivery Lead signals. That points to tighter delivery control around jobs, candidates, clients, and service outcomes.

Our read

Yolk is scaling a service model that covers On Demand, retained, contract, temporary, Talent Team, and executive work. The delivery signal tells us the hard part is not only finding people, but keeping every handoff clear. A slow job page, weak form tracking, or delayed status report can hide where a role is stuck. If that stays manual, consultants lose time and leaders lose a clean view of service quality.

What we'd do

We would start with a dedicated squad under your Product or Operations owner, not a long fixed rebuild. The squad would improve live web and delivery flows while your team keeps recruiting.
