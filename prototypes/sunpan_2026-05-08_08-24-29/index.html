Our read

The hiring signal tells us SUNPAN is scaling customer success while the Customer Portal rolls out. The hard part is likely the handoff between trade login, CRM, WMS, claims, and sales reps. If those flows stay split, reps spend time chasing order status instead of helping trade customers. The business risk is slower portal adoption and more support load during growth.
