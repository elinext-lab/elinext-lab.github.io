Rule pack builder

Urgent result / specimen exception pack

  • Trigger logic Potassium > 6.0 or delta > 1.5, amended report, or barcode mismatch from collection flow.
  • Scoped routing After-hours routes to RN queue first for Site 02 and Site 04; daytime routes directly to physician group.
  • Evidence policy Require read-back capture, locked reason code, acknowledgement timer, and final disposition before close.
  • Surface parity One rule pack deployed to LabMD iOS, Android, and web so policy drift does not reappear by device.
