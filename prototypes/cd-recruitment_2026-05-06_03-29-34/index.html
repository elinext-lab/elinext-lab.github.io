Engineering support for CD's EMEA public sector push

CD already knows how to build SaaS GTM teams. The next step is making the internal tools, data, and reporting ready for more complex client work.

Why we're here: we saw the Regional Sales Manager role for Public Sector and Defence across EMEA. That move can raise pressure on search workflow and client reporting.

Likely pressure
  • More audit-ready search notes
  • Cleaner CRM and ATS data
  • Faster AI, Cyber, and Infrastructure matching

Our read

That role says CD is pushing into a more complex buyer segment, not just adding another sales seat. Public Sector and Defence searches need cleaner proof of source, status, region, and stakeholder history. If those details live across CRM notes, ATS fields, and search sheets, recruiters spend time fixing data instead of moving candidates. The risk is slower shortlists and less clear weekly reporting for clients.
