Cyberr can scale trust without slowing product delivery

Why we're here: saw Cyberr carrying SOC and system security roles while expanding the cyber network. This brief focuses on the product work behind that shift: verified profiles, faster matching, and resilient operations.

Our read

The signal says Cyberr is moving from a focused network into a broader hiring and services platform. SOC and system-security roles mean more job data, more matching rules, and more trust checks around real cyber work. Your site already names the core product pieces: verified profiles, trust score, credential validation, AI matching, Ghost Mode, and a freelance marketplace. The bottleneck is keeping those pieces consistent across the public site, app, job pages, and marketplace. If that layer drifts, recruiters lose confidence and strong cyber pros see noise instead of value.

Where we'd start

Route hygiene

The /freelance route currently lands on a 404 while /marketplace-info presents the freelance offer. Redirect it and track clicks from old indexed links before adding more demand campaigns.

First view speed

Lighthouse found large avatar and hero images driving slow first view on the homepage. Ship responsive image variants before the next content push.
