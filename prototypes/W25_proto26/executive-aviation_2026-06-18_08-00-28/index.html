Our read

The hiring signal says Executive Aviation is scaling the front line, not just filling seats. Your Customer Service Agent listing names check-in, self-serve kiosks, boarding, airline policies, and crew coordination. That is a lot of live information for new people to learn fast. If station tools stay split, managers answer repeat questions and agents wait for context. The business risk is slower onboarding and more small handoff misses during busy turns.
