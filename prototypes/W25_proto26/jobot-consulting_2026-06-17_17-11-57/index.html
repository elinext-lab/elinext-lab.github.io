Our read

The interim IT Manager role points to a short-term operating gap, not a vague software need. Jobot is also shipping more AI into hiring, with Jax 6.0 adding Fast Track and natural language search. Jeni adds AI calls soon after apply, so candidate trust depends on clean handoffs and clear status. If IT work piles up, small issues in onboarding, alerts, access, or mobile performance can hurt a very human promise.
