Tighten Leeds' IT hiring and candidate intake path

A short brief on the IT Operations Manager signal, the public candidate form, and a practical way to support recruiters while the internal operations load grows.

Saw Leeds is hiring an Information Technology Operations Manager. That points to a need for steadier systems, vendor control, and a cleaner intake path for clients and candidates.

Our read

The IT Operations Manager role says Leeds is putting more weight on systems, support, security, and vendor work. That matters because your public site is also a live intake path, not only a brochure. The Candidates page asks for role type, name, phone, email, and a PDF resume through WPForms and reCAPTCHA. If those leads do not enter the right workflow fast, recruiters carry the gap by hand. A small operations layer can make every new role and resume easier to track.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

The candidate form accepts only PDF resumes and starts with a broad Business or Prospective Candidate choice.

Useful next step

Add a discipline field before upload, so IT resumes and client requests route to the right recruiter.

What we noticed

Lighthouse flags slow mobile loading on the Candidates page, where first contact happens.

Useful next step

Compress the hero image and trim unused scripts before adding more forms or routing logic.
