Recruiting platform brief

Help Cypress turn hiring demand into cleaner client handoffs

Cypress already sells speed and care. The next gain is making each web handoff easier to track, from client intake to job flow and enablement.

Why we're here: we saw current signals around a Strategic Client Director, a PMO Director, and an LMS enablement role. That points to client growth, delivery load, and training needs.

Our read

The signal says Cypress is scaling more than recruiting volume. It points to client ownership, program control, and LMS-backed field enablement. The likely bottleneck is the handoff between client intake, job publishing, recruiter follow-up, and training content. Cypress already uses a web mix that includes Avionte jobs, Herefish, Bullhorn login, and Gravity Forms. If those paths stay split, leaders get less visibility and teams spend more time asking for missing context.

Useful next step

The Workday intake flow mixes two jobs.

Recruiting and payroll need different data. Split the form by service type, then ask extra fields after the first fit check.
