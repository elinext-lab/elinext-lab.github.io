Saw MHC is hiring a Head of IT while your portal and driver app carry more fleet work. This brief shows where an outside squad could help Olaf's team keep delivery steady.

Why we are here

The signal points to cloud, security, provider and delivery load landing on a small IT team.

Our read

The Head of IT search points to a broader platform handover, not just a hiring gap. MHC asks that role to own Azure, AWS, IT security, providers, SLAs, migrations, budgets and a five-person team. At the same time, the Fleet Management Portal promises fleet data, documents, reminders, fuel cards and charging support. MHC GO adds damage reports, delivery status, mileage updates and account-team contact. If this work waits for one hire, roadmap items can slow and Olaf's team carries too much context alone.

Where we'd start

Observation

Map one data path first: contracts, documents, mileage, damage reports, delivery status and reminders across ERP, Salesforce, portal and MHC GO.

Next step

Clean the public web queue too. Oversized images and unused JavaScript are low-risk wins while the deeper platform backlog is shaped.
