Our read

The PMO and Azure/M365 roles show demand for senior delivery, not simple resume flow. Hamilton Barnes is matching fiber, telecoms, and cloud programs where timing and follow-up matter. The candidate account already promises Shortlisted Jobs, Job Applications, and Job Alerts. If status data sits across job search, consultants, Teamtailor, and timesheets, a good candidate can still feel lost. That can slow high-value placements and add manual reporting work for the data team.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

The mobile job-search page loads a heavy script stack and Lighthouse flagged slow rendering. First step: trim unused scripts, defer non-core form assets, and set a mobile budget for the apply path.

Useful next step

The login page sells applications, alerts, and saved jobs. Map those events to recruiter ownership and follow-up rules, so no senior candidate repeats the same story twice.

What we'd do

Use a dedicated squad under your product or data owner. A six-week pilot would ship weekly demos and leave your team with tests, metrics, and a clearer delivery map.
