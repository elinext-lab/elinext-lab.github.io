Our read

The hiring signal says Bondaval is scaling BondavalOS, not just filling a seat. The hard part is keeping policy logic, buyer data, and release quality moving together. BondavalOS already flags cover breaches, tracks buyer profiles, and helps teams fulfil policy obligations. If that work piles onto one small team, new cover flows and broker needs can slow down. The risk is not a weak idea; it is a strong product needing more delivery capacity.
