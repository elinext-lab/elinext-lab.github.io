Our read

Ace is hiring more Artisans while the public site asks customers to use Get an Estimate and Find an Ace Handyman. That tells us growth is reaching the field and the intake path at the same time. The likely pinch point is not code headcount; it is repeatable office workflows, booking handoffs, and clear job data for each Craftsman. Dispatch has already helped Ace scale fast, including adding multiple offices per week. If the digital flow lags, local teams can lose speed right when new demand arrives.
