Our read

That hiring signal says Eventus is scaling the team behind its long-term care model. Greg sits where that growth meets HIPAA, identity, vendor apps, and daily support. Eventus already routes families through a patient portal, clinicians through PerfectServe support, and care teams through AI-assisted documentation with Ambience. The bottleneck is not one app. It is secure handoffs across portals, intake forms, EHR-adjacent tools, and new staff. If those handoffs stay manual, onboarding slows and M&A waves create more support load.
