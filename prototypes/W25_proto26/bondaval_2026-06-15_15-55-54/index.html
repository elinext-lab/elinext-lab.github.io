Our read

Your Full Stack Engineer search says BondavalOS is moving into a heavier platform phase. Bondaval now sells credit insurance and non-payment guarantees through BondavalOS. The Swiss Re partnership pushes the same platform across the UK, EU, US, and Canada. That puts more pressure on APIs, policy flows, and accounts-receivable data. The hard part is not one screen. Where we'd start

Map the service contracts first

Your platform config points to separate services for order, auth, docs, credit, messages, and accounts receivable. Before adding more features, we would map those API contracts and add tests around credit and AR paths.

Make login lighter before demos

The mobile check on the platform login was slow and flagged a large JavaScript payload. Split the first login bundle before broker demos, then measure the change on real devices.
