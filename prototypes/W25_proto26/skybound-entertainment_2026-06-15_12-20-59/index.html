Our read

That Senior Producer role says Skybound is scaling game delivery across partners, not only one title. The hard part is keeping creative, tech, QA, marketing, finance, and external teams moving from the same plan. The Walking Dead: Season One still has huge fan love on mobile, but recent App Store reviews mention restore-purchase failures, crashes after episode buys, and blank screens.
