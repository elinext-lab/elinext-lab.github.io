Our read

The hiring signal says Qliro is scaling checkout, not only filling seats. Gen 4 adds Fast Track, validation, upsell, loyalty, and multi-channel checkout. Merchant onboarding also needs self-service setup for payment options and checkout configuration. That creates a bottleneck between product ideas, merchant integrations, and release checks. If the gap stays, pilots slow and support sees more edge cases. The recent WooCommerce thread around TWO Invoice shows why callbacks and order state need tight tests.

Where we'd start

Observed

First, test duplicate webhook paths around TWO Invoice and Qliro for WooCommerce. One merchant thread says orders were held after repeated payment_complete events.

Next step

Next, tighten mobile payment confidence before more checkout features ship. Recent Android reviews mention freezes, missing reminders, and users unable to pay.
