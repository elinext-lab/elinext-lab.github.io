Our read

The Back End Developer signal says Gravitas is adding product-side capacity, not just a brochure update. Your public platform already carries job search, CV upload, region handoff, and employer enquiry flows. Those flows support the same promise your site makes: fast, niche talent with strong compliance. The hard part is keeping matching, contact preferences, and consultant follow-up clean as more candidates arrive from web, events, and direct outreach. Trustpilot feedback praises tailored check-ins, but negative reviews mention ignored email and timesheet friction. If that gap stays, strong recruiters spend time repairing trust instead of moving candidates.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Lighthouse shows the jobs and CV pages are slowed by large JavaScript and late page paint on mobile. Start by profiling the SourceFlow and Next.js search path, then split non-critical scripts away from job search and registration.

Next useful step

Public reviews point to channel preference and follow-up gaps. Add a simple contact-preference and task-sync trail from CV upload to CRM, so email, SMS, and consultant actions stay aligned.
