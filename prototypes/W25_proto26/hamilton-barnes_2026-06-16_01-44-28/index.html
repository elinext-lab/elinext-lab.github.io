Our read

Senior Director of PMO and Senior Cloud Architect signals show demand around delivery control and Azure/Microsoft 365 work. For Hamilton Barnes, that likely means more high-value roles entering the funnel while consultants still need fast candidate follow-up. Your site already supports job search, CV dropoff, vacancy upload, and salary tools. The bottleneck is not only traffic; it is moving each lead from web form to consultant action without delay. If that handoff slips, strong candidates feel ignored and urgent cloud roles lose momentum.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

The Job Search page loads heavy on mobile. Move search filters and core results ahead of tracking scripts, then re-test the first result view on a slow phone.
