Help SiGMA Match keep pace with Foundation growth

We saw SiGMA Foundation hiring for Partnerships and Operations. When that role lands, partner intake, donor reporting, and event follow-up will need the same care as SiGMA Match.

Why we're here The signal points to more partner work, more proof needs, and more pressure on creative tech delivery.

Our read

That hire says the Foundation is moving from one-off projects to repeat partner work. SiGMA Match already carries a busy event flow. It covers ticket login, agendas, meetings, floor plans, QR exchange, badges, feedback, and recommendations. The risk is that new Foundation programs add another set of portals, reports, and approvals. If the creative team ships all of it alone, launches slow and partner data splits across tools.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

SiGMA Match is a PWA with offline cache and push. The login route still ships a large bundle. Split map, meetings, and profile tools after login.

Useful next step

The Foundation asks visitors to bring projects forward. Map that intake to one small partner CRM before the new operations lead arrives.
