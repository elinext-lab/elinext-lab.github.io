Our read

The bottleneck is not more traffic

Your Software Engineer hiring signal points to active demand around delivery capacity. For Realtime, that demand usually sits inside client roles, candidate flow, and fast qualification. The public site already supports this with Job Search, The Realtime Way, RealView, and Codility checks. The bottleneck is that the job board and intake flow depend on a heavy WordPress stack, SenseHQ embeds, and client-side listings. When senior engineers are deciding where to apply, slow or fragile pages can cost trust before a recruiter speaks to them.

Where we'd start

Two fixes before adding more tools

What we noticed

On the Job Search page, the listing shortcode only appears after JavaScript runs. The no-script copy tells candidates that listings need JavaScript.

Useful next step

Add a small server-rendered first page and keep filters usable without the full widget stack. Defer chat, reviews, and tracking until jobs are visible.

What we'd do
