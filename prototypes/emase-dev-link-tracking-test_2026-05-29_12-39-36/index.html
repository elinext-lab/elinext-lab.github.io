Our read

The signal says you are checking whether campaign events can be trusted. The hard part is not one link click. It is the full chain from email client to Lemlist, redirect, UTM, and lead record. If that chain is unclear, sales follow-up can be based on false activity. A small QA sprint can make the evidence repeatable.
