What we'd do

Start with one controlled pilot

A dedicated squad under Diana's team. Ten weeks. One active build or facility flow.

Engagement model: a dedicated squad working under your internal owner, with weekly demos and clear rollback options.

01

Map the real flow

Map how engineering, outfitting, and facility requests move between teams, sites, and co-makers. Mark duplicate entry, offline files, and approval waits.

02

Build one shared workbench

Build one shared workbench for change requests, document status, and punch items, so blockers appear before they affect fabrication or sea-trial dates.

03

Connect the current systems

Connect core systems and role-based alerts for engineering, procurement, and site leads. Keep every handoff searchable, dated, and easy to audit.

04

Prove the model

Run the pilot on one yacht or facility flow, with weekly demos, baseline timing, and rollout notes for the next stream.
