Our Read

Your public signal says change hiring is active now. Your own transformation page says these roles cut across firms and are hard to fill. At the same time, the candidate journey is split between the main Totum site and a separate Firefish portal. When change mandates rise, that split slows role launch, candidate capture, and reuse of market insight. The result is more manual work for consultants and a weaker first impression for scarce change talent.
