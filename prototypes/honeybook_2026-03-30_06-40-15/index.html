Why Nimrod should care

This is a data problem disguised as a UX problem.

The mobile layer gets calmer only if the scoring, traceability, and feedback loops underneath it are strong. That makes this a natural conversation for a Director of Data Engineering, not just a design review.

01

Explainability event model

Each suggestion should carry reason codes like stale thread, proposal reopened, invoice pending, or repeated scheduler views. That makes the AI legible and measurable.

02

Feedback that actually teaches the model

Approve, edit, snooze, and mute are not just UI actions. They are high-value labels that can reduce weak follow-up spam and improve confidence thresholds over time.

03

Support-ready diagnostics

When users question a suggestion or a payment flow, support should see the full event trail instantly. That reduces handoffs, shortens resolution time, and protects trust.
