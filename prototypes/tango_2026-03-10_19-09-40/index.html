Current vs improved system

The product gap is small on the surface and deep underneath: visible receipts, session evidence, and state-safe recovery flows.

Current

  • User spends coins without a trust checkpoint.
  • Claims start from empty support text.
  • Refund state is invisible after submission.
  • Support absorbs ambiguous, emotional complaints.

Improved

  • Pre-purchase verified-live badge reduces uncertainty.
  • Every paid interaction gets a session receipt and event trail.
  • Claims route through structured reasons with auto-attached evidence.
  • Users see SLA and outcome status inside the app.
