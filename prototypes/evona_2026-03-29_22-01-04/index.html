What to build

Four focused moves, not a full product rewrite

This concept stays close to FRACTION's existing product language and public app routes. The goal is to tighten shortlist trust, review speed, and collaboration.

01
Explain the match

Show why each executive is on the shortlist with sector overlap, operator proof, availability, and red-flag notes.

02
Collapse the review loop

Replace scattered email and Slack feedback with one mobile approval lane for founders, colleagues, and investors.

03
Expose response ownership

Make recruiter status visible so candidates and clients always know what happens next and who owns it.

Client-side routes already exist

`network/search`, `shortlists`, and `vacancies` indicate a natural mobile review layer.

Member-side depth is real

`applications`, `cv-analysis`, and `offsite-vacancies` suggest product maturity beyond a landing page.

Category skepticism is high

Recruiting buyers are increasingly skeptical of AI labels without clear workflow value.

Best mobile wedge

Do not port the whole platform. Own the decision moment where trust and speed matter most.
