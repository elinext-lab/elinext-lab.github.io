Proposal brief

Keep cloud delivery clear while Sentia scales service ownership

Sentia is pushing public, private, and sovereign cloud work for customers that need safe operations. We would help Thomas' public cloud team keep delivery clear while new demand grows.

Saw the Service Delivery Manager hire. It points to more customer portfolios, service plans, reporting, and follow-up across Technology and Operations, not just one more account role.

Our read

The signal says Sentia is growing service ownership around complex cloud customers. That means delivery managers need cleaner data from operations, change work, budgets, and improvement ideas. Public cloud demand is also moving toward Danish data control and AWS European Sovereign Cloud. If the handoffs stay manual, customer meetings slow down and improvement work gets lost. Thomas' team needs a repeatable delivery layer around managed cloud services.
