Selected opportunity

Governed activation control plane

Instead of letting SMS, email, push, device status, and clinician messaging behave like separate products, give the member one explicit setup path and give ops one auditable source of truth.

  • Member-set channel preference and reminder intensity
  • Pre-session device readiness and recovery guidance
  • Resilient clinician thread with visible response expectations
  • Audit-ready events for consent, escalation, and handoff
