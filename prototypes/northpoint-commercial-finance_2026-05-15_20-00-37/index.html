Proposal brief by Elinext

Make sales support faster as Northpoint keeps growing

Northpoint helps dealers and manufacturers move inventory with flexible finance. The next step is making the support work behind every application easier to see, route, and finish.

Saw you are expanding Sales Support. That usually means the team is handling more applications, documents, portal questions, and CRM updates than the current workflow can absorb.

Sales support is a system problem. More staff helps. Better intake, status, and routing makes each hire count.

Our read

The Sales Support hire tells us Northpoint is scaling the work behind deals. That work likely sits between Apply Now, AccessCompass, sales reps, and credit teams. If those systems do not share status well, support people chase documents and retype updates. That slows decisions and makes a personal lending model harder to protect. A small automation pilot can remove the repeat work first.
