Our Read

The hiring signal says Syntax Europe is pushing managed-services growth in the industrial SME market. When that happens, the weak spot is rarely core technical knowledge. It is the handoff between sales, consulting, project delivery, and service management, especially when customers expect one clear portal for tickets, billing, and multi-cloud control. If that layer stays uneven, renewals slow down, account teams spend too much time explaining status, and operations leaders carry avoidable noise.
