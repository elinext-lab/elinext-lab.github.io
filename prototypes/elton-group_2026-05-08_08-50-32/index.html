Make credit control easier to scale

A short proposal for a finance workflow pilot that connects ERP data, customer risk, and collections work in one place.

Why we're here: we saw ELTON is expanding its Credit Control and Collections team. That is often the moment when better tools help new hires move faster.

Focus for the pilot
  • Show overdue invoices with customer, owner, and order context.
  • Cut manual reports across finance, sales, and warehouse teams.
  • Give managers a clear view of payment risk.

Our read

ELTON is scaling a finance function that sits close to sales, logistics, and customer service. The hard part is not only hiring a collections specialist. It is giving that person trusted data from ERP, invoices, orders, and customer notes. If the workflow stays manual, follow-up slows down and cash risk is harder to see across countries.

What we'd do

We would run a scoped six-week project, with weekly demos and one ELTON owner for finance decisions.
