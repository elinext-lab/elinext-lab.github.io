Proposal brief

Make dealer onboarding easier for the sales support team

A focused pilot for Salesforce flow, AccessCompass handoffs, and credit package checks.

Saw Northpoint is hiring Sales Support Coordinator capacity to board dealers, grow receivables, and manage work in Salesforce. Here is how we would help that hire move faster.

Our read

The hiring signal says Northpoint is scaling sales support around dealer boarding and line growth. That work depends on Salesforce, AccessCompass, credit packages, field sales, and operations moving in one clear path. The bottleneck is likely not effort; it is status, document, and follow-up clarity across teams. If that stays hard, new dealers can wait longer, and sales leaders get less visibility into which accounts need action.
