Our read

The routing signal tells us Salesjobs is seeing roles where place, route, and territory rules matter. That is harder than a normal sales hire, because recruiters must match skills, city coverage, travel limits, and client rules. If those checks stay manual, shortlists slow down and quality can vary. A small data layer can keep your promise of fast finalistas while helping recruiters see the right fit sooner.
