Help Street scale Italy without digital bottlenecks slowing service

Street already has a clear brand, strong city energy, and delivery reach. The next risk is making every new store launch with the same data, order, and stock rhythm.

We saw the Italy operations hiring signal. That usually means the store plan is moving faster, and the digital layer must keep pace.

Our read

The Italy hiring signal tells us Street is building a repeatable regional model. The hard part is not the burger brand. It is keeping ordering, Glovo handoffs, store data, stock planning, and opening checklists in sync. If those systems stay split, each new store adds manual work. That can slow launches and hide demand patterns from the operations team.
