Keep BGE2 Character production moving with less drag

A focused six-week support plan for Lucie Goiran and the Montpellier leads, built around character pipeline clarity, asset checks, and partner handoffs for System 3.

We saw Ubisoft is hiring a Team lead Character for Beyond Good & Evil 2. That points to a real need for structure around Character production, quality, and delivery.

Our read

The role tells us Montpellier is moving more Character production into a clearer delivery frame for BGE2. That usually means leads need cleaner gates between concept, high-rez, low-rez, variations, animation needs, and Voyager integration. If those gates stay manual, Lucie and the senior team spend too much time chasing state, partner feedback, and late asset fixes. The business risk is simple: delivery slows while public attention on the game stays high.
