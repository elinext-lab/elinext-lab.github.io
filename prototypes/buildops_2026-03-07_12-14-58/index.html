App Loading & Crashes

The most common complaint: the app takes too long to load, crashes mid-workflow, and freezes when typing notes.

10-Minute Photo Uploads

Technicians report waiting 10+ minutes to upload a single photo. Photos save to the phone gallery, creating thousands of orphaned images.

"Want to upload pictures? You can wait 10 minutes! Want to upload videos? Nope!"
— App Store, 1★, Oct 2025

Time Tracking Bugs

Clock-in/clock-out records don't match actual time worked, creating payroll disputes and billing inaccuracies.

"I will clock into a job at 7am, clock out at 10am, and I only have 1 hour on the clock."
— App Store, 1★, Oct 2025

Too Many Taps

Simple tasks like closing a work order require too many steps. The UX was designed for office data collection, not field speed.

"Obviously designed by an app developer that has never been in the field. This app has made an enjoyable job miserable."
— App Store, 1★, Oct 2025

No Offline Support

Technicians can't clock in or access job details without cellular signal — a problem on commercial job sites like basements and server rooms.

"Very annoying to arrive on a job site with poor Internet service and try to find a signal so you can clock in."
— App Store, 4★, Aug 2025

AI Features Miss the Mark

AI features optimize for office reporting, not field productivity. Technicians say AI additions make the app worse, not better.

"They've made this app worse too by putting pointless AI things I don't need in the field."
