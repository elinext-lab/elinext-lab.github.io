Proposal brief

Scaling data accuracy across LTVCo's public-record product portfolio

LTVCo helps people use public records across people, vehicle, property, phone, and family data. At that scale, trust depends on current facts and clean access flows.

Why we're here: we saw the Automotive Fact-Checker signal for Bumper content. It points to a wider accuracy moment across data, copy, reports, and review queues.

Our Read

The fact-checker signal says LTVCo is tightening trust controls across Bumper content. That same need likely touches BeenVerified searches and report flows. Manual review protects the brand, but it does not scale alone. If stale facts or broken paid access slip through, trust drops fast. The right move is a validation layer that finds issues before reviewers spend time.
