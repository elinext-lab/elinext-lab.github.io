Elinext mobile concept for DigiSign

What if DigiSign made assurance feel predictable on mobile?

The opportunity is not to weaken biometric signing. It is to tell users exactly what will be checked before they start, keep each verification step legible on a phone, and package the final proof for ops the moment the document closes.

Simeon, your public remit points to enterprise reliability, compliance, and platform scale. This concept is aimed at the overlap between user drop-off and buyer-owned operational drag in a BVN/NIN + liveness environment.
