Helping SkyWater finish senior finance searches with less friction

SkyWater already wins on trusted search and local fit. This brief focuses on the web steps that turn a hard finance search into clear candidate action.

Why we're here: We saw the Senior Manager -- CFO Services search for a growing M&A/PE advisory client. That role needs a clean apply path and steady recruiter follow-up.

Our read

The CFO Services search shows SkyWater is helping a client build a higher-value finance advisory line. The public post names FinOps, PE-backed firms, value creation, and portco growth. That makes candidate fit narrow and every handoff important. SkyWater's job board uses Matador Jobs Pro on WordPress, with filters and resume upload. If that flow feels slow or unclear, strong finance leaders may drop, and recruiters chase missing details.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

On the Senior Manager page, candidates upload a resume and accept privacy terms before they submit. Test the file state, mobile labels, and error copy first.

Useful next step

SkyWater reviews praise fast follow-up, but one client mentioned tech issues with assessments. Put assessment status beside recruiter notes, so clients do not ask where a candidate stands.

What we'd do

Run a six-week pilot as a dedicated squad under SkyWater's recruiter operations owner. The goal is simple: improve the job search and apply flow without replacing trusted recruiter work.
