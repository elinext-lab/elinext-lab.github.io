Proposal brief by Elinext

Turn new math expertise into tools partners can use

Instruction Partners already helps leaders move from strong materials to strong daily instruction. The next step is a cleaner digital path for content, tools, and partner feedback.

I saw you are hiring for math content expertise. That usually means more playbooks, model lessons, and training assets, which need a smooth way to reach partners fast.

What this moment points to

More math-specific professional learning
More diagnostic tools and playbooks
More demand for reusable digital resources

Our read

The Math Content Specialist role says Instruction Partners is adding subject depth, not just more delivery capacity. That growth will create more partner-ready math resources, rubrics, pacing guidance, and training materials. The bottleneck is likely the handoff from expert content to a digital system that leaders can search, use, and update. If that stays manual, rollout slows and your team spends too much time packaging the same knowledge again.
