Proposal brief for Agilus

Extra delivery capacity for Agilus public-sector technology work

Agilus already connects skilled people with employers across Canada. This brief shows how Elinext can add vetted software delivery capacity behind your technology and government accounts.

Saw Agilus is hiring a Procurement Advisor. That usually means partner quality, vendor speed, and contract control are getting more important for service delivery.

The moment we see

  • Procurement is becoming a sharper part of delivery.
  • Federal work needs clean proof and careful partner control.
  • Technology demand is visible across jobs and client services.

Our read

The signal tells us Agilus is tightening how outside partners are chosen and managed. For federal and enterprise technology work, the hard part is not finding names; it is proving skill, security, and fit fast enough. If that work sits only on internal recruiters and procurement, client bids can slow down. A vetted engineering partner can give Michael a clean path for delivery without adding hiring load.

What we'd do

We would run a dedicated squad under Agilus service delivery. Agilus keeps the client relationship; Elinext supplies the engineering pod and delivery proof.

Team shape

One delivery lead, two senior backend engineers, one React or Angular engineer, one QA automation engineer, and DevOps support.
