Proposal brief for Fundraise Up

Keep donor data moving while your data team scales

Fundraise Up is growing a data-rich fundraising platform across AI, payments, CRM sync, mobile giving, and donor analytics. That scale needs reliable pipelines and clean reporting paths.

We saw Fundraise Up hiring a Senior Data Engineer for ClickHouse, Airflow, Kafka, S3, MongoDB, and data warehouse work. That is a clear signal of data platform pressure.

Our read

The Senior Data Engineer role says Fundraise Up is scaling the data layer behind donations, AI, CRM sync, and Insights Dashboard. The hard part is not one pipeline. It is keeping many donor events clean across currencies, subaccounts, payment methods, and campaigns. Reviews also ask for deeper reporting and smoother integrations. If this stays blocked, product teams lose speed and customers wait longer for trusted fundraising data.
