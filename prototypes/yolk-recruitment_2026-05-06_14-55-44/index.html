Tighten Yolk's tech hiring platform while demand keeps moving

Yolk already wins by knowing the tech market. We would help make the web journey faster for candidates, clearer for consultants, and easier to report to clients.

Our read

The delivery roles tell us Yolk's Tech & Digital desk is handling complex client demand. That puts pressure on search, CV capture, alerts, and shortlist updates. If those steps stay split across tools, consultants spend time joining dots. Candidates wait longer, and clients see less of the work Yolk is already doing.

What we'd do
