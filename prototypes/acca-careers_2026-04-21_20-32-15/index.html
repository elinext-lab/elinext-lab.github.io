Our read

Where the pressure is now

ACCA Careers now spans live jobs, job alerts, Role Insights, Career Navigator, and Recruiter Centre. That breadth helps reach, but it can blur ownership across recruiter and jobseeker flows. Reddit posts also show users questioning entry-level fit and qualification labels. If that noise grows, faster users go back to LinkedIn or other job boards.

What we'd do

One focused six-week platform sprint

We would run a six-week dedicated squad under Laurence's roadmap, with weekly demos.

01

Map the live path

Map the route from recruiter post and approval to job alert, job detail, and completed apply event, so delay points become visible.

02

Trim mobile friction

Tighten mobile search, filter, and detail-page behavior around the Madgex core, trimming heavy scripts and calming layout shifts on small screens.

03

Clean up fit signals

Add clearer rules for level, skill, and qualification labels, so trainee users see better fit and recruiters avoid mixed requirement signals.

04

Measure the weak spots

Give Laurence one reporting view across alerts, approvals, CV actions, and apply starts, then back it with QA checks before release.
