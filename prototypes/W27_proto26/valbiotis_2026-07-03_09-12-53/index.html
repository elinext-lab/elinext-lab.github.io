Our read

The capital raise says Valbiotis is moving from lab proof to commercial scale. The hard part is not only selling more boxes in France. It is keeping one product story clear across Valbiotis PRO Cholesterol, Santé Métabolique, Cardio-Circulation, and new CBEC channels. Your Santé Métabolique page must explain Totum 63, dosage, and lifestyle advice without slowing purchase. Verified shoppers already say they need to read a lot before choosing a product. If that stays hard, paid traffic and partner launches can leak value.

Where we would start

Two small moves can reduce friction before any large rebuild.

Product choice

Short path, deep proof

Split Santé Métabolique into a short buyer path and a deeper science path. Keep Totum 63 proof nearby, but do not make every buyer read it first.

Repeat orders

Loyalty and fulfillment tests

Test a simple loyalty credit and review multi-product packing rules. Shoppers already mention price and separate parcels, even when delivery is on time.
