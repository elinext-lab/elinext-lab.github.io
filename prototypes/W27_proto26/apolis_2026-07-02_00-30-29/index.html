Our read

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Make every full-stack role page state the client context, stack, contract length, and screening band at the top. Reddit threads show candidates ask whether Apolis roles are direct or client placements.

Next useful step

Trim the public site's global script load on hiring pages. The WordPress theme loads animation and slider libraries even when a simple job flow needs speed.

What we'd do

Engagement model: a dedicated squad under Apolis's Product Owner or delivery lead. Start with a six-week pilot, then keep or resize the pod based on the first workstream.
