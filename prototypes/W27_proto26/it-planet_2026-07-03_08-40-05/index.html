Scaling IT-Planet after the merger without slowing delivery

Why we're here: we saw the Java Developer signal, then checked IT-Planet's public hiring and merger news. This brief shows where a small software squad can reduce delivery load.

IT-Planet is hiring developers while merging three IT consulting units.

The public promise is fast matching, often within two days.

Our read

The Java hiring signal says IT-Planet is adding delivery capacity, not just filling one seat. Your site also lists Full Stack, Back End, and DevOps roles. At the same time, Planet Group says IT-Planet, Bizztalent, and Centric staffing are moving under one IT-Planet name. The bottleneck is likely handoff between hiring, client demand, and backoffice systems. If that stays loose, the 48-hour matching promise gets harder to protect.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Client and vacancies pages carry the growth load.

Mobile checks show slow page paint and blocking scripts. Start with image, script, and form-tracking cleanup on those paths.

Useful next step

Backoffice harmonization needs a field map first.

Map candidate, client, and assignment records across IT-Planet, Bizztalent, and Centric before adding more automation.
