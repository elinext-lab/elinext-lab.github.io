Our read

The hard part is trusted data at speed

The hiring signal says Phocas is adding engineering capacity, not just replacing hands. Phocas AI, 200+ integrations, and BI plus FP&A all depend on clean source data. Public G2 and Capterra feedback praises ease of use, but also points to data freshness, export limits, and setup friction. If those issues grow with more AI use, teams may lose trust in answers and fall back to Excel or ERP screens. The business risk is slower adoption after a big product push.

Where we'd start

Two checks worth doing first

Data freshness in every answer

G2 review themes mention real-time data limits. Add last-sync, source health, and permission scope beside key dashboards and AI answers.

Connector setup smoke tests

A Reddit user praised NetSuite setup but saw Sage access delays. Make ERP access, sample data, and upload checks visible before build starts.
