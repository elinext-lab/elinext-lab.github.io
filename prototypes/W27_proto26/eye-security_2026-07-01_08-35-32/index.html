Our read

The Series C says Eye Security is moving from fast growth to platform scale. Cyber Guard now has to support more markets, deeper integrations, and AI-assisted response while the SOC stays expert-led. The bottleneck is likely the customer and partner product surface around Eye Portal, Eye Log, onboarding, reports, and recommendations. A public Capterra review praises Eye.Security but says the SOC Dashboard has limited functionality. If that gap stays, more customers may need human help before they see clear value.
