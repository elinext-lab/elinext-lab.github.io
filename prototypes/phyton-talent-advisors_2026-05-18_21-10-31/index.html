Our read

Phyton is adding KYC support, which points to more financial services work and more client onboarding. The hard part is not finding analysts only. It is keeping documents, checks, risk notes, and status updates clean across every handoff. If that stays manual, turnaround slows and the best analysts spend too much time on repeat checks instead of judgment.
