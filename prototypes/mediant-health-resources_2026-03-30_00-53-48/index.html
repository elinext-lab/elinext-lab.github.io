How It Works

Three moves, no replatform story

This concept keeps Mediant's existing public site in place and adds a mobile candidate-ops product where the value is actually created.

1. Capture Role-fit intake built for healthcare IT Candidates see what matters for the role immediately: Epic module depth, FHIR workflow experience, NAC or CMDB exposure, and compliance-sensitive project signals.
2. Secure Safer credential and document handoff Instead of loose recruiter follow-up, Mediant gets a guided mobile checklist for resumes, certifications, client summaries, and next-step tasks.
3. Shortlist Mobile recruiter queue for Jon's team Recruiters and ops leads can park, escalate, or shortlist candidates quickly, with the strongest profiles rising first for security-heavy roles.
4. Trust Visible status instead of silence The candidate sees the stage, owner, and expected next action. That is operationally simple and brand-protective at the same time.
