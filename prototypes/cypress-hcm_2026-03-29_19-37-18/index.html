What changes

Four moves that make the experience feel productized

Unified sign-in

Workers stop guessing which system owns their credentials. Cypress becomes the identity layer, not the referral layer.

Deadline visibility

The Monday timecard cutoff becomes a live status card with reminders, approvals, and escalation instead of a buried FAQ fact.

Pay confidence

Pay stubs, tax forms, and direct-deposit changes become discoverable from one home screen even if the source system stays the same.

Support orchestration

One issue intake means Cypress owns the recovery path when account, payroll, or document access breaks.
