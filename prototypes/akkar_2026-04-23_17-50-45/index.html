What We'd Do

A short build for client flow

We would start with a scoped six-week project. The goal is simple. Give each Client Partner one live view of account health, worker progress, and next actions.

01

Map the real handoff

Map the full path from client brief to placed worker. Find duplicate steps across sales, recruitment, visa, payroll, and support.

02

Build one account workspace

Build one shared workspace with milestones, risks, owner notes, and worker status. Keep the human touch, but remove inbox chasing.

03

Join forms and data

Connect forms and documents so clients, recruiters, and operations share the same facts. No re-keying across WordPress, forms, and onboarding tools.

04

Give leaders calm reporting

Set weekly views for account load, onboarding blockers, and time to start. Leaders see risk early, before clients feel it.
