Our read

The likely bottleneck is the handoff between recruiter habits, job data, Bullhorn, Herefish, and the public job board. If that handoff stays manual, AI tools can add noise instead of speed. The business risk is simple: recruiters lose time, candidates feel friction, and new growth gets harder to support.
