Why This Matters For Lisa

Lisa Molyneux's remit sits at the intersection of operations, planning, and execution. A clean mobile workflow turns scattered launch activity into trackable states leadership can actually manage.

Facility readiness

See which hospitals are blocked on VAC, storage prep, coding, or training instead of relying on rep updates.

Supply confidence

Support load

Prototype Scope

Three mobile workflows worth testing first.

Each module is commercially useful on its own and together they create the kind of case-readiness system that helps a first launch scale without adding more admin drag.

Module 1

Account Readiness

Track VAC progress, hospital champion, storage requirements, and first-case checklist for each facility.

Module 2

Case Operations

Surface shipment ETA, batch reservation, field owner, and OR-day status in a single mobile timeline.

Module 3

Coding + Follow-up

Deliver the right reimbursement packet, log support notes, and trigger post-case follow-up without another spreadsheet.

Prototype thesis

Humacyte does not need a generic mobile makeover. It needs a focused mobile layer that helps hospital teams and field teams move from interest to ready-to-use with less friction.
