Proposal brief for Realty Trust Group

Make RTG PinPoint ready for faster analyst work

Saw the RTG Analyst hire in Knoxville. That points to a bigger data push, so this brief focuses on the pipelines, reports, and clean views that would help the new role move faster.

  • ProductRTG PinPoint and DataBridge

Our read

The Analyst hire tells us RTG is putting more weight on portfolio insight, not just property work. PinPoint already gives clients a single trusted source, while DataBridge points to the harder part: moving lease, accounting, market, and operations data into clean views. If that flow stays manual, the new analyst may spend time checking spreadsheets instead of finding savings, risk, and care delivery answers.

What we'd do

Engagement model: a dedicated data squad under RTG's product and operations owner, run as a 6-week pilot with weekly demos.

Team shape

Small enough to move fast, senior enough to own data quality.
