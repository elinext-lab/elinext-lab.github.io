Our read

The hiring signal says RBCx is scaling the venture suite, not just adding headcount. For Mydoh, that likely means more parents and teens touching payment flows, ID checks, wallet funding, and family invites every day. App reviews show these are the moments that create support tickets when they fail. If product fixes lag behind CX growth, the team spends more time explaining bugs than improving retention.
