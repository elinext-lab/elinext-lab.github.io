Where the pressure likely sits

The signal says your Europe team is handling more complex product hiring, not only more roles. The hard part is keeping briefs, shortlist quality, recruiter outreach, and client updates aligned across several tools and teams. When that flow breaks, strong candidates wait, consultants switch context, and time-to-submit rises just when clients expect faster delivery.

What We'd Do

A focused six-week delivery pilot

We would start with a dedicated squad under your product owner or operations lead. The aim is simple: make Technology Jobs, recruiter workflow, and client status feel like one system.

Team shape and stack

Milestones

01
Map the current hiring flow

Map the path from hiring brief to live role, shortlist, and client update across HubSpot, Bullhorn, SourceWhale, and Technology Jobs.

02
Tighten the candidate journey

Tighten the mobile job flow, search filters, and employer microsite blocks so candidates move faster and consultants get cleaner source data.

03
Add clear delivery controls

Add vacancy status rules, shortlist checks, and recruiter alerts so Europe delivery leads spot blocked roles before clients chase updates.

04
Ship with reporting from week one

Ship weekly with QA, analytics, and DORA-style flow reports that show submission speed, handoff gaps, and release risk early.
