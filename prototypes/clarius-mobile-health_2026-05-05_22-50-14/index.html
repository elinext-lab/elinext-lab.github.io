Our read

Adding inside sales means Clarius is increasing lead conversion and market coverage. Public reviews point to update, WiFi, and support friction, while Clarius is also adding AI, billing, and enterprise scanner management. If these paths feel uneven, deals can slow and sales teams spend time explaining product risk instead of clinical value.
