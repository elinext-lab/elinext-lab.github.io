What Ships First

A lean release that proves the thesis fast.

This does not need a giant platform rewrite. The fastest path is a mobile companion that sits on top of the existing EVONA and FRACTION workflow.

1. Verified outreach

Recruiter identity, role summary, fit score and response expectation shown in a persistent mobile view.

2. Shortlist collaboration

Founders and investors approve candidates, leave notes and avoid losing context across calls, email and CRM.

3. Meeting certainty

One-tap booking, backup channel, reminders and ownership of the next action reduce the exact trust gap showing up in public chatter.
