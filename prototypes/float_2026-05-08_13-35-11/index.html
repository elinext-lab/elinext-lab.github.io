Proposal brief for Float

Help Float reduce support load as customer adoption grows

Float has a broad finance platform: cards, Bill Pay, reimbursements, business accounts, and mobile workflows. That breadth is powerful, but it raises the cost of each customer handoff.

Saw Float is hiring a Customer Success Manager. That suggests onboarding and adoption work is growing, and engineering can remove repeat friction before it becomes more tickets.

Focus for a six-week pilot Mobile admin gaps Bill Pay and reimbursements Accounting workflow QA

Our read

The CSM hire says Float is scaling customer adoption, not just adding support seats. The hard part is helping finance teams move through setup, mobile approvals, Bill Pay, reimbursements, and accounting sync without confusion. Public app comments also point to admin work that can feel desktop-bound. If those gaps stay open, Customer Success spends time explaining product edges instead of growing account value.
