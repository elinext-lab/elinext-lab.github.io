Our read

The hiring signal says Softwire is adding delivery capacity, not only filling a seat. SWIFT also asks the team to ship insurance sales journeys, self-service policy work, claims documents, payments, and analytics on each client stack. That mix can stretch senior people, because every insurer has its own rules and connectors. Clutch clients praise Softwire's low-oversight delivery, but one asked for more access to senior advisory support. If delivery load grows faster than senior review, project starts can slip or key people get pulled into too many details.
