Why we're here

Our Read

Hiring for process development tells us the operating layer needs work. Saarioinen Ammattilaiset already connects product facts, recipes, concepts, chat, and partner ordering. That breadth helps customers, but it creates many handoffs between teams. When handoffs stay split, launches take longer and errors travel farther.

What We'd Do

One short process project

We would run this as an eight-week scoped project. One weekly demo. One owner on each side.

1
Map the real content path

Trace how product facts move into Tuotehaku, Reseptihaku, Kokoset, AI answers, and Kespronet. Mark duplicate work, slow approvals, and risky manual edits.

2
Create one trusted publish layer

Build a small integration layer for clean product payloads. Publish the same truth to public pages, campaigns, and partner feeds.

3
Make updates easier for teams

Redesign the editor flow for Food Service and marketing teams. Update recipes, allergens, assets, and concept pages with fewer waits.

4
Add release and QA checks

Add checks around links, product facts, image weight, and content mismatches. Catch issues before assortment changes or campaigns go live.
