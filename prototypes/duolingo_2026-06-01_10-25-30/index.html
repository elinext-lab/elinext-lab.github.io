Our read

Duolingo is scaling from a language app into a wider learning system. That means every change touches habits, streaks, audio, and trust. Public reviews show friction around Energy, course moves, speech checks, and chess performance. If those states feel unfair or unclear, loyal learners slow down, complain, or leave before new subjects can grow.
