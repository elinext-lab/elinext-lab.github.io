Where support pressure can build

Quote and client intake Visa and document status Payroll and PRO/GRO updates Support tickets and handoffs

Our read

The support hire says Terracotta is adding care for daily systems, not just more people. Your public site sells EOR/PEO, staffing, recruitment, payroll, PRO/GRO, and business setup. Those services create many small status questions after the quote form. If document, payroll, and onboarding data stay split across tools, support becomes a chase role. The business risk is slower client updates and less room for Amaar's commercial growth work.
