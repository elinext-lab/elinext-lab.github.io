Beyond Good & Evil 2

A focused tools squad for Beyond Good & Evil 2

Beyond Good & Evil 2 lives across art, UI, production, engine, and partner flows. The work needs clear tools that help leads decide fast during a busy build.

Saw the Team Lead Character role in Montpellier. It points to character production that needs clearer state, faster handoffs, and less manual load on senior art and UI leaders.

Why we are here Use a small external squad to make production signals visible before they slow delivery.

Our read

The Team Lead Character role says character production needs more structure. Beyond Good & Evil 2 also has rare scale and many handoffs. That can put extra load on UI, art, tech, and partner teams. If status lives in meetings and scattered tools, delivery calls get slower. A clear internal layer can turn asset state into faster decisions.

What we'd do

We would run a dedicated squad under your product, production, or tools owner for a six-week pilot with weekly demos.
