Where we'd start

Two useful fixes first

What we noticed

Search listings need a fallback

The Search Jobs page tells no-JavaScript visitors that listings cannot be viewed. Add a server-rendered list of priority roles and JobPosting schema.

Next step

Speed up the first useful screen

The mobile audit points to render-blocking styles and heavy images. Defer non-critical CSS and compress large hero assets before traffic hits.

What we'd do

A quiet partner squad

Engagement model: a dedicated partner squad under Connect Search's account lead. Six-week pilot, then month-to-month if it helps active searches.

Team shape

One technical lead, two senior engineers, one QA lead, and one business analyst for role checks and client calls.
