A tighter delivery system for Energize's US SAP push

We saw the Head of SAP search and Jay's role in the US Talent-as-a-Service push. That usually means more demand, more handoffs, and more pressure on speed, follow-up, and clean consent.

Why we're here

Your public site already sells scale. The next step is better software behind that promise.

What this is

A simple proposal for workflow, follow-up, and platform support. Not a fake product redesign.

Our read

You are growing US SAP work, not just filling one role. Your site already runs a wide vacancies platform across SAP, cloud, and robotics. When that grows fast, recruiter effort can spread thin, follow-up slows, and data rules get harder to keep clean. Public reviews still praise the team, but a few recent comments mention ghosting and GDPR worries. That is the kind of small gap that can hurt a premium brand.

What we'd do

We would use a dedicated squad under your PO or ops lead. The shape is a six-week pilot with weekly demos, then a clear keep-or-stop call.

Team shape

1 Tech Lead 1 BA / Product Analyst 2 Senior Full-Stack Engineers 1 QA Engineer

Relevant stack

Map live recruiter flow
