Steady Ingenuity delivery while backend demand grows

Eigen Ingenuity now spans dashboards, Excel, mobile access, historian data, and AI-linked workflows. That is strong product momentum, but it raises the load on backend delivery.

Why we're here: we saw Eigen hiring a Senior Back End Developer, with AI and full-stack roles also open. That usually means the product roadmap is moving faster than the core team can safely absorb.

Our read

Eigen looks to be scaling Ingenuity from a strong platform into a broader operating layer. The signal is not just one backend hire; it is backend, full-stack, and AI work landing at the same time. Ingenuity now has an Excel Add-In, mobile access, historian APIs, and new AI optimisation work with Pontem Analytics. The risk is that customer work slows when each surface needs its own tests, releases, and support paths. If this bottleneck stays, senior engineers spend too much time on release safety instead of product decisions.
