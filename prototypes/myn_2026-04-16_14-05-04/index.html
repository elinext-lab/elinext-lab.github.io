Growth now depends on simpler handoffs

The public product estate is already broad. Myn sells through the main site, portal, help centre, talent-pool site, and academy. That means the platform is growing fast, but product truth is split. The bottleneck is not feature shortage. It is the cost of moving recruiters, clients, workers, and partners through one source-to-pay flow without confusion. If that stays heavy, adoption slows and support work grows.
