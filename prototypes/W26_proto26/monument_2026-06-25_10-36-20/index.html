Our read

The funding signal says Monument Technology is moving from proof to scale. The hard part is not only new features. It is keeping savings, ISA transfers, service chat, and core-banking handoffs calm while more clients join. Monument's own site names Mambu, Salesforce, digital channels, and one platform SLA. That mix can create brittle release paths if tests and runbooks lag. If this stays manual, support teams feel the load first.
