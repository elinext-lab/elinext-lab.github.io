Our read

Togetherwork is moving a beloved camp product from one-off custom work toward a true SaaS model. The role description says the platform covers staff recruitment, parent registration, payment plans, bunk assignments, activity scheduling, and launch learning. That is a heavy surface for one delivery lane. Public reviews point to the same risk: camp teams like having registration, health forms, Bunk1 photos, and store work in one place, but they also call out scheduling, reports, support handoffs, and bugs. If this load stays uneven, summer fixes will keep stealing time from scalable roadmap work.

Where we'd start

Critical path

Mobile media
