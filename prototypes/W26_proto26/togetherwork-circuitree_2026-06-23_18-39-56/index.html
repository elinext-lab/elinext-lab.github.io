Keep CIRCUITREE delivery steady through peak camp season work

CIRCUITREE already handles the camp work that matters. That includes registration, health forms, payments, staff, cabins, schedules, and parent updates. The next push is about safer release flow and clearer user paths.

Saw the Delivery Product Manager role for your camp platform. It points to a real need. Keep vision, backlog, and engineering moving while summer support stays heavy.

Our read

Your hiring signal says the camp platform needs tighter product ownership. The role joins strategy with backlog work, sprint readiness, and story definition. That points to a team balancing roadmap bets with summer support load. CIRCUITREE reviews praise registration, payments, HR, and camp store in one place. The weak spots are report tuning, admin learning curve, and slow feedback loops. If those stay open, releases can slip and camp operators feel the strain first.
