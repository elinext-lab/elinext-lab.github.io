Our read

Fieldway is moving from a strong regional specialist into a wider compliance platform. The Axis CLC deal keeps the Fieldway brand and team, while adding more scale. Your site also shows Fieldway Compliance, Consulting, and Coatings as one joined offer. The hard part is making client intake, site evidence, surveys, and reporting feel just as joined. If that layer stays manual, managers carry the load and service quality gets harder to prove.

Where we'd start

Survey flow

Your public customer survey asks about scope, needs, cleanliness, courtesy, time, communication, quality, and satisfaction. Turn it into a shorter mobile flow, then route weak feedback to a manager within one working day.

Portal health

The public app hostname returned a service-unavailable page in our check. If it supports any portal, add a health check, clear error page, and uptime alerting.

What we'd do

Run a six-week pilot with a dedicated squad under your operations owner. The goal is one small delivery layer, not a large rebuild.
