Our read

Your Delivery Manager signal reads like a push for steadier product delivery, not just more hands. The camp platform adds pressure because one release can touch staff recruiting, parent registration, payment plans, cabins, and activity schedules. Bunk1 reviews also point to photo loading and message flow issues after updates. If those changes ship without strong checks, support gets the pain first. A delivery lead helps, but they still need a squad that can clear old risk while new work moves.
