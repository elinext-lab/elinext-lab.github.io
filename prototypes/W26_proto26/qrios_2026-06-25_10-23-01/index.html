Our read

That role says QRIOS is building a deeper Life Science talent bench. The bottleneck is not only sourcing; it is conversion across several systems. Candidates move from qrios.se to Teamtailor, then consultants use the Intelliplan portal. The Process Developer advert asks for GMP, tech transfer, CAPA, and validation skills.
