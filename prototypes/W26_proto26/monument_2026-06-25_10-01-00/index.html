Our read

The funding says Monument is moving from a strong bank app to a wider BPaaS delivery race. That puts pressure on cloud operations, data migration, and support paths at the same time. Your own Data & Migration role names pipelines, rehearsals, cutover, incident management, and sign-off. App reviews are mostly strong, but the weak spots sit in sensitive flows: ISA transfers, chat resume, lockout, and maintenance messages. If those stay manual, growth can turn rare issues into noisy incidents. The business risk is slower client launches and more support load.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Recent Android reviews mention crashes, lockout, and maintenance after rejected transactions. First step: tag those flows in telemetry and link them to support tickets.

Useful next step

An App Store review points to ISA transfer and chat resume friction. Add a weekend-safe transfer status screen before more ISA volume arrives.
