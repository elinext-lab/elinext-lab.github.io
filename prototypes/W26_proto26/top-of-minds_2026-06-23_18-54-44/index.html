Keep technology searches moving while the platform catches up

Saw Head of Technology and Head of Information Technology signals under Top of Minds. This brief shows how a small technical bridge team could protect vacancy delivery, candidate flow, and platform trust.

Our read

Saw the Head of Technology signal in business terms: Top of Minds is carrying more complex technology-leadership searches. That is more than a role to fill. It asks your own platform to support fast vacancy pages, confidential search work, employer-branding stories, and candidate data. Your site already calls the search approach tech-enabled and shows IT, Product, Scale-up, and Technology paths. If the product layer moves slowly, searches can still launch, but reporting and follow-up become harder than the brand promises.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Vacancy pages are campaign assets.

Your homepage combines filters, localized paths, images, and consultant contacts. Build a release checklist for each new search page: media, metadata, forms, tracking, and accessibility.

Next useful step

Technology-role pages need mobile speed.

The mobile audit flagged heavy media and unused scripts. Start with CTO and Head of Technology templates, then add checks before each campaign goes live.

What we'd do

Use a dedicated squad under your PM or Head of Technology. The pilot runs six weeks, with a demo every week.
