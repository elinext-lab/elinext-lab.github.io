Our read

Your signal shows a bank scaling Savings delivery and BPaaS at once. Monument now has active Savings roles across engineering, product, and design. Monument Technology just raised seed funding and added Castle Trust Bank. That creates two delivery fronts with one trust standard. Reviews praise simple setup, yet flag ISA transfers, chat, device login, and crashes. If these edges stay unmanaged, growth can turn support into product debt.

Where we'd start

Observed

Keep the one-device rule, but add a clearer trusted-device recovery path. Reviewers describe approval loops across iPhone and iPad, so test step-up checks with biometrics.

Next step

Treat ISA transfer and chat faults as one service journey. A daily review across app, servicing, and partner APIs would show where clients wait.

What we'd do

Engagement model: a dedicated squad under Shaun's product and platform leads.

Team: 1 delivery lead, 2 mobile engineers, 1 backend engineer, 1 QA automation engineer, 1 DevOps engineer.

Pilot: six weeks, with a weekly demo and clear go or hold gates.
