Our read

The Wiley acquisition means Emerald now has more reach, more buyers, and more platform pressure. Emerald Insight already moved to Silverchair in July 2025, with ICE content, redirects, merged accounts, Research Pass changes, and administrator records in play. That is a lot of access logic for librarians, authors, and researchers to trust. If small issues stay in sign-in, downloads, or discovery metadata, support load rises while Americas teams try to grow. The risk is not launch failure; it is slower adoption when each institution has a different access path.
