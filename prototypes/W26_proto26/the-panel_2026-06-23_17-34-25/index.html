Why we are here

Keep technology work moving during CIO change

We saw the fractional CIO, interim CIO, and Head of IT and Digital Transformation signals. That usually means the roadmap needs calm delivery while the long-term leader is still being settled.

This brief is about the live recruitment platform: Job Search, Upload CV, sector pages, and the handoff into CRM and recruiter workflows.

Our read

The CIO signal points to more than one hire. It suggests The Panel wants firmer control over systems, data, security, and delivery. Your Technology & Transformation page already covers product development, QA, DevOps, cloud, IT security, and infrastructure. That is the same work an interim CIO will need to steady first. If those paths wait for a permanent hire, job search, CV intake, and CRM handoff can keep adding small risks. The cost is not only technical debt. It is extra work for recruiters and weaker candidate flow.

Where we would start

Noticed

Your Technology & Transformation page created sideways scroll in a phone-width check. The useful next step is a mobile pass on all sector pages, starting with filter chips and job cards.

Noticed

Upload CV depends on forms, consent, CAPTCHA, and likely CRM handoff. Test it with consent denied, blocked CAPTCHA, missing file, large file, and a valid submission.

What we would do

Use a dedicated squad under your interim or incoming CIO. Keep it short, visible, and easy for the new leader to inherit.

Team shape

One tech lead, one full-stack engineer, one integration engineer, one QA automation engineer, and a part-time UX designer.
