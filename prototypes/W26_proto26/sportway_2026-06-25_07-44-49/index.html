Our read

Your recent capital raise says Sportway is moving from strong regional scale to global repeatability. The Studio Automated acquisition also brings more AI production software into the core platform. That is useful, but it can slow teams if data models, release paths, and client setups stay different by market. Playsite already connects federation schedules to live and on-demand games, payments, and white-label sites. The next bottleneck is making each new sport and country feel like configuration, not a custom project. If that slips, expansion wins can turn into delivery load for product, support, and local operations.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Map the route from federation schedule to Playsite event, official scoresheet, overlays, and automated highlights. A thin contract test around that route would catch country-specific breaks before match day.

Treat Studio App and Team Account as shared workflows, not separate apps. One mobile QA matrix for capture, tagging, replay, and account handoff would protect releases as markets scale.
