Our read

The logistics role tells us Schmersal is adding people around material flow, not just sales. At the same time, the new webshop now carries about 15,000 safety, control, and automation products in around 20 countries. That puts pressure on product data, stock data, and order status. If these feeds sit in separate tools, new hires spend time chasing answers. The risk is simple: faster customer demand meets slow internal handoffs.

Where we'd start

What we noticed
