Our read

Monument is moving from one strong bank app into a platform used by other banks. The funding signal says the team must scale delivery, not only maintain the app. That is hard when the BPaaS promise includes native mobile, web, Mambu, Salesforce, support tools, and one SLA. Public app reviews praise simple saving flows, but recent notes mention crashes, maintenance states, lockouts, and ISA transfer stress. If these edge cases grow with new client banks, platform teams lose time to support and release risk.
