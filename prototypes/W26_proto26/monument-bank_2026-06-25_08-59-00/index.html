Our read

Monument is scaling a technology-led bank, not just adding more savings products. The app is the front door for clients who open accounts, move money, and speak to real people. Public reviews praise the simple flow, but recent comments point to crash, lockout, maintenance, transaction, and ISA transfer edge cases. The Membership FAQ also says Lifestyle and Net Worth are being redesigned after the March 2026 decision. These paths need steady releases, clear states, and calm support handoffs.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

Android reviews name crash, lockout, and maintenance states. Add a release-health lane before feature work, with auth recovery and payment failure checks.

Useful next step

A reviewer asked for future-dated withdrawals, and another flagged a Cash ISA transfer delay. Map those edge cases before the next sprint.

What we'd do

Start with a dedicated squad under Monument's product owner: mobile lead, two app engineers, backend engineer, QA automation engineer, and fractional DevOps. Run a six-week pilot with weekly demos.
