Bridge port data work before the BI hire lands

Saw you are hiring a Senior Business Intelligence Developer. This brief shows where a small Elinext data squad could protect momentum while your permanent hire comes onboard.

Focus: PortVan eHub, live data feeds, and BI-ready reporting.

Our read

The Senior BI hire says the port is turning more operations into trusted data products. PortVan eHub already gives truck wait times, rail crossings, vessels, and alerts to port users. The next hard part is not one more chart. It is clean data flow across terminals, rail, marine systems, and public tools. If that flow stays fragile, dashboards lag behind the work. Then planning, community updates, and partner trust all take longer.

Where we'd start

What we noticed

The GPS Dashboard showed a reload error below rail crossing rows. Add feed health checks, so one broken call does not hide status.

Useful next step

App reviews mention webcams, vessel-at-anchor crashes, and train-crossing glitches. Turn those into data-contract tests before new BI reports depend on them.
