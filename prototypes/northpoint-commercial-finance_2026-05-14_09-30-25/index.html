Make dealer onboarding faster before sales support gets busier

A focused Elinext proposal for Northpoint Commercial Finance: reduce manual credit-package work, connect portal handoffs, and help Chantal's team move dealers from application to funding with less chase work.

We saw Northpoint is hiring a Sales Support Coordinator. That role touches Salesforce, credit packages, underwriting, operations, and dealer follow-up - a clear sign that deal flow needs cleaner support paths.

Sales support growth Dealer application flow Credit package work

Our read

The new Sales Support role says Northpoint is adding capacity around dealer onboarding and line changes. The hard part is likely not one more form; it is the handoff between Salesforce, the Application Portal, AccessCompass, credit files, and customer follow-up. When that path stays manual, coordinators spend time checking packages instead of helping dealers fund faster. That can slow the personal, flexible service Northpoint is known for.
