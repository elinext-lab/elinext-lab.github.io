Our read

The hiring signal says Ownr is adding customer help while also needing senior product engineers. Ownr's public pages show a wider job: registration, incorporation, minute books, bank handoff, and yearly compliance. Public users ask what happens after setup, how renewals work, and why the bank step appears in the path. If these questions stay in support queues, the CX team grows but product learning slows. Platform Ops can turn that feedback into safer releases and fewer repeat issues.
