📱 Competitor Advantage

LeafLink, Nabis's primary competitor with 12,000+ users across 30 markets, already has native mobile apps on both iOS and Android. Every day without a mobile app, Nabis risks losing retailers who prefer the convenience of mobile ordering — especially field sales reps who need to demo products and place orders on the dispensary floor.

⚡ The 30x Opportunity

A native mobile app would unlock capabilities impossible on the web: push notifications for order updates and promotions, barcode scanning for instant product lookup, offline catalog browsing for warehouses with poor connectivity, and biometric login for secure, one-tap access. With 3,500+ orders per week, even a 5% increase in order frequency from mobile convenience could mean significant revenue uplift.
