Why we're here: we saw the Senior Software Engineer signal tied to your recruitment flow. If that gap belongs to a client, bridge capacity can protect their roadmap while your search continues.

Our read

The signal says a team needs senior engineering capacity now. Your public site shows this is usually a client role, not an internal build need. That creates a hard gap: the client still has product work, code reviews, and releases while the search runs. If delivery slows, the client feels the risk before the hire is ready.
