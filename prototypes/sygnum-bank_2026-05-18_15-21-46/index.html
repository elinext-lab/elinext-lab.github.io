Proposal brief for Sygnum Bank

A core platform squad for Sygnum's next markets

Why we're here. Saw Sygnum is hiring for Core Platforms International. That points to API-first banking work across markets, with operations, risk, and engineering needing one stable path.

Focus: digital asset banking, B2B access, and tokenized finance.

Moment: new regions, new partners, and more platform run work.

Our read

The Core Platforms International role says Sygnum is taking its banking backbone into more markets, not just adding features. The hard part is the space between APIs, ledgers, risk checks, local rules, vendors, and Singapore handovers. If that work stays inside one stretched team, releases slow and run work competes with new B2B and tokenization demand. The fix is a small delivery squad that improves platform flow without breaking bank-grade control.
