Our Read

The hiring signal says Germany is pushing harder on managed services. When sales adds accounts and service teams add SLAs, CxHub becomes mission-critical. Billing, provisioning, backup, and support status must stay clear. If those flows feel fragmented, teams fall back to tickets and side chats. That slows delivery and makes service quality harder to protect.
