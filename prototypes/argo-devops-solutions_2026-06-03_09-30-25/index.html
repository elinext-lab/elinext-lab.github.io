Our read

Argo looks ready to push more owned product work, not just client delivery. The Software Engineer signal points to pressure across secure coding, requirements, testing, and cloud release work. WhereBIST already has a clear market: desks, rooms, parking, custom rules, and business intelligence for hybrid offices. The hard part is trust in daily use. If staff ignore bookings, or if no-show rules are unclear, the product can look weak even when the core platform works.

Where we would start

Next step
