The Solution

What a Modern Eltropy Mobile App Looks Like

Built with Flutter for iOS + Android from a single codebase. Designed for credit union staff on the go.

📱

Flutter = iOS + Android

One codebase, two platforms. No more maintaining separate apps (or abandoning one). Ship updates to both stores simultaneously.

🔔

Real Push Notifications

Instant alerts when members message. No more checking the browser on your phone. Loan officers respond in seconds, not hours.

🔌

Offline Message Queuing

Draft and queue responses even without connectivity. Messages send automatically when back online — essential for field visits.

🎯

Role-Based Views

Lending, collections, sales, and support teams each see their relevant conversations. Smart filters reduce noise, increase focus.

📊

On-Device Analytics

Response time, conversation volume, member satisfaction — all visible at a glance. No more asking support for basic reports.

🔒

Compliant by Design

NCUA-compliant messaging with audit trails, message archiving, and role-based access controls built into every interaction.
