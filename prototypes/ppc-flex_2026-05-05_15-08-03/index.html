Make invoicing keep pace with PPC Flex growth

Saw you are expanding the accounts receivable invoicing team. That often means invoice data, approvals, and collections are growing faster than back-office tools can absorb.

We would help finance reduce manual touches, connect core tools, and keep plant-to-cash handoffs clear.

Our read

PPC Flex has grown through plants, markets, and recent deals. The AR hiring signal says finance is taking more daily load. That load likely crosses Radius, Open Accounts, Docuware, Concur, and plant data. When shipment, PO, and invoice data do not meet fast, cash slows. The fix is not more forms; it is a cleaner flow.

What we'd do

Engagement model: a scoped end-to-end project, run as a six-week pilot with weekly demos.

  1. 1
    Map the handoffs

    Map invoice handoffs from order, shipping, PO match, and customer billing across the tools your finance team already uses.

  2. 2
    Connect core tools

    Build connectors that move invoice status, proofs, and exceptions between ERP, document storage, and accounting screens.

  3. 3
    Add document checks

    Add OCR checks for PDFs and scanned paperwork, then route unclear items to the right owner.

  4. 4
    Pilot and prepare rollout

    Pilot one plant or customer group, measure manual touches, and prepare rollout notes for other locations.
