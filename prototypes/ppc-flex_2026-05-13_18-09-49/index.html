Our read

PPC Flex is adding finance capacity while the business runs more plants and supplier paths. The hard part is likely not one invoice screen. It is the handoff between shipments, customer website receivables, returns, plate invoices, subcontract inventory, and AR aging. If that stays manual, close work gets slower and small data errors can delay cash.
