Napier is moving a large water programme while residents still need safe, steady service every day.

Saw the Assets & Services Delivery Lead role. It points to job costing, planning, productivity, and stronger control of people, plant, and assets.

Our read

The role tells us Napier wants stronger control over daily asset work, not just more reporting. Water Services is also preparing for a wider regional model, while projects keep moving. The bottleneck is likely work visibility across jobs, costs, assets, and service levels. If that stays spread across teams and tools, delivery risk grows and leaders spend more time chasing status.
