Our read

The hiring signal says Artemys is carrying more live cloud work, not just more projects. Omni365 bundles backup, monitoring, security, and user support into one monthly offer, so every new customer adds recurring run-load. When delivery ownership is thin, small incidents sit longer, handovers stay in people’s heads, and senior engineers get pulled from build work into coordination. That slows new work and makes service quality harder to hold steady.
