Our read

The hiring signal tells us Zillion is scaling debit-card dispute operations for a banking client. That work is not simple ticket handling; it touches Visa and Mastercard rules, RDR, member contacts, evidence, general ledger entries, and case systems. The bottleneck is likely the handoff between people and tools, especially when every case needs clean notes and fast proof. If that stays manual, local staff carry more pressure while cycle time and compliance risk rise.
