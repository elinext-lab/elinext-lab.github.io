Tech support for Street's next Italy growth step

We saw Street hiring a Head of Operations / COO in Italy. That role usually needs clean data, fast order flows, and clear tools for every new restaurant.

Street is growing from founder-led restaurants into a larger QSR group. We would help keep the same quality control while stores, delivery channels, and city teams multiply.

Our read

The COO signal says Italy is becoming a core market, not a side launch. Street already routes orders through Glovo, Uber Eats, and its own site. That can work early, but it splits store numbers, delivery issues, and customer data. If the new operations lead lacks one clear view, growth gets harder to manage and founder quality checks become a bottleneck.
