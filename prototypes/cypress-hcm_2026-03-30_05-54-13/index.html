Mobile Opportunity

What if Cypress turned jobs, onboarding, and timecards into one mobile contractor workspace?

Cypress already exposes the right building blocks publicly: a career portal, payroll and compliance services, new hire portals, and web-based time and expense management. The gap is the mobile handoff between them.

This concept focuses on the real friction public users can feel today: portal switching, login/code confusion, and low trust in time-entry flows.

Public Signals

Where the friction shows up

The opportunity is supported by Cypress's own product language plus the proxy mobile feedback around Bullhorn time and expense.

No clear official app

US App Store and Google Play checks did not surface a clear Cypress-owned mobile app. The current experience is web-first.

Payroll flow is portal-based

Cypress explicitly advertises new hire portals and web-based time and expense management, which is functional but easy to fragment on mobile.

Bullhorn proxy reviews are weak

Public complaints center on login failure, 4-digit code confusion, false internet errors, and low trust during payroll-sensitive tasks.

What public evidence suggests

Cypress homepage links directly to a Bullhorn universal login.
The jobs entry point is a separate `career-portal/#/` SPA.
Payroll and compliance copy still frames time and expense as web-based.
Bullhorn Android reviews repeatedly mention code confusion and unstable access.
Prototype Goal

Reduce delivery drag by unifying the contractor journey. The point is not to replace Cypress's stack overnight. The point is to give workers and account teams one dependable mobile front door.

Concept Focus

A mobile layer built for enterprise delivery

This concept targets the narrowest high-value wedge: better worker follow-through with less support intervention.

Passwordless entry

Use assignment-aware access so contractors never guess which staffing ID, branch code, or tenant they need.

One action queue

Surface job status, onboarding tasks, missing documents, and timesheet deadlines in one prioritized home screen.

Pay clarity by default

Let workers see shift history, approved time, and upcoming pay status without calling support.
