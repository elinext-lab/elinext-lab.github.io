Why this fits Eltropy

The product is already broad. Mobile should reduce complexity, not expose it.

Eltropy now spans messaging, voice, video, AI, payments, collections, and 50+ integrations. The mobile layer has to act like a confident operating system for staff, not a thin companion app.

Reliable conversation kickoff

Fix the first-click failure that surfaced in the App Store and replace it with a deterministic, channel-aware launch experience.

  • Member lookup tied to CRM and core-banking context
  • Safe defaults for SMS, RCS, voice, and video
  • Draft persistence that survives app switches and poor network

AI with compliance guardrails

Use AI where it compounds trust and speed instead of adding more surface area for staff to manage.

  • AI-generated first reply based on live account context
  • Sensitive language checks before send
  • Escalation path into secure callback or video banking

Operational visibility for admins

Public SaaS reviews already hint at reporting and template friction. Mobile should feed the system of record, not create side-work.

  • Sent / failed / retried counts exposed automatically
  • Template distribution without support intervention
  • Retry queue diagnostics for branch and collections teams
