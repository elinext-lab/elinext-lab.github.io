Proposal brief for Roc Technologies

Build Coria delivery capacity without slowing secure service work

A focused proposal for Roc Technologies as Coria grows from a strong public-sector proof point into a repeatable delivery and service model for new customers.

We saw Roc hiring a Principal Service Delivery Manager. That points to more strategic accounts, more SLA pressure, and more need for clear service evidence while Coria scales.

Our read

The service delivery role tells us Roc is growing strategic customer work, not just filling a support gap. At the same time, Coria Digital Cost Assurance is moving from strong project proof into a repeatable SaaS offer. The hard part is keeping integration, release, and audit evidence clean for each buyer. If that stays manual, pilots slow down and service teams carry extra pressure.

What we'd do

Use a dedicated Elinext squad under Roc's product owner or PM. Start with a six-week pilot and weekly demos.
