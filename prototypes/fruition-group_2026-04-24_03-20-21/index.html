What We'd Do

A six-week workflow reset

We would run one scoped end-to-end project with a Fruition operations owner. The goal is simple: fewer handoffs, cleaner status, and safer changes on the live workflow layer.

1

Map the real journey

Map candidate, consultant, and contractor journeys across sign-in, alerts, applications, timesheets, and reports. Mark every blocker, repeat step, and missing owner.

2

Clean the handoffs

Review the Eploy flow, portal forms, and report logic. Keep what works, then remove duplicate entry and status gaps that slow consultants.

3

Ship focused fixes

Improve account setup, application progress, shortlist updates, and online timesheets. Add clearer views for delivery teams and client-facing managers.

4

Harden weekly release work

Add QA checks, release gates, and one small delivery dashboard. Track failed steps, stalled applications, and report delays before clients chase updates.
