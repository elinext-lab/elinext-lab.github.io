Proposal brief for Sidetrade

Helping Aimie's sales push land faster with less product drag

Sidetrade is moving from O2C software to AI agents that act inside finance work. That shift needs clear buyer proof, fast pilots, and stable product paths.

We saw Sidetrade hiring enterprise BDRs in Calgary and Dublin while Elaine is leading product marketing for Aimie. More pipeline means more demos, more pilots, and more pressure on product teams.

Our read

The BDR hiring tells us Sidetrade is scaling enterprise demand for Aimie and the O2C Intelligence Platform. The hard part is turning new interest into pilots that are easy to explain, easy to start, and safe for finance teams. Public pages also show heavy ERP, CRM, billing, banking, and communication links. If product and sales assets lag, good deals can slow during security, integration, and pilot setup.
