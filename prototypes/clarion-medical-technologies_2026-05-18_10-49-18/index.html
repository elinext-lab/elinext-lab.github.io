Our read

Your signal says Clarion is adding injectable sales capacity while the online store is still growing into a full reorder channel. Existing customers can reorder many aesthetic brands, but some injectable lines are still coming, and account changes still route to support. That can put new consultants between clinics, order data, and product rules. If the store, training, and CRM flows do not move together, sales time goes into manual follow-up instead of practice growth.
