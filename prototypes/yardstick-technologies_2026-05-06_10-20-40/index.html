Our read

The vCIO hire says Yardstick is growing strategic account work. Total Network Care, weekly account meetings, and Customer Business Reviews all need clear notes, risks, and next steps. The hard part is keeping RoadMaps, project hours, TAM handoffs, and client goals in one repeatable flow. If that stays too manual, strong account work can depend on one person. Deals may slow down, and technical teams may get less context than they need.

What we'd do
