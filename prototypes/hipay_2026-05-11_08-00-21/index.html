Proposal brief for HiPay

Protect payment speed while compliance work grows

HiPay gives merchants one place to manage online, in-store, and remote payments while support teams need clear answers.

Saw HiPay is hiring a Compliance Officer while your team owns Payment Performance. That mix often means more checks, reports, and audit paths inside flows that must stay fast.

This page is not a product mockup. It is a focused plan for extra engineering capacity under your own team, aimed at compliance load, payment tests, and release safety.

Our read

The Compliance Officer hire suggests HiPay is adding more control around payment risk, audit, and reporting. That is good for trust, but it can add work inside authorization, refund, AML, and 3DS2 paths. If this work lands only on senior payment engineers, performance work can slow. Merchant support also needs clear transaction history when a payment is pending, blocked, or refunded.

What we'd do

A dedicated squad works under your PO or PM for a six-week pilot. The goal is simple: protect payment performance while compliance changes move faster.
