Our Read

Where the pressure likely sits

BM&A is hiring around consolidation and finance systems, not only advisory roles. That suggests client demand is rising while reporting, review, and training work must stay consistent across offices and teams. When this grows faster than the internal product bench, tools become a bottleneck: fixes wait, onboarding slows, and specialists return to manual follow-up. The business risk is simple: strong finance expertise gets pulled into software friction instead of client delivery.
