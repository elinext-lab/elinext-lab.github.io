Make Gotion's European after-sales loop ready for BESS scale

Saw Gotion hiring an after-sales engineer in Gottingen. That points to a real moment: local BESS production is growing, and service standards now need to grow with it.

Our read

Gotion is moving from product launch to field scale in Europe. The signal is not just one hire. It says after-sales must cover training, complaints, customer care, R&D, and quality. That is hard when BMS, HMI, PLC, thermal, and fire systems all create service data. If the loop stays manual, field issues take longer to reach engineering.
